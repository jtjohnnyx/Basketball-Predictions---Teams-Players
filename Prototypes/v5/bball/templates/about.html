User's Guide

Welcome to NBA Wizard, below are the supported features and how to use them!

  • Get Team Info:
    • Select the team and the season you want to view and click submit

  • Get Player Info:
    • Select the player you want to view and click submit

  • Get Standing:
    • Select the team and the season you want to view and click submit

  • Find Past Games:
    • Automatically displays the most recent game played in descending order

  • Find Upcoming Games:
    • Automatically displays the upcoming matchups, by clicking the compare button, you can compare team stats and view their odds of winning
